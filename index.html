About G.S.R.T.C

The Gujarat State Road Transport Corporation (G.S.R.T.C) is a passenger transport organization providing bus services both within Gujarat and neighboring states. Established in 1960, G.S.R.T.C is known for its reliable, safe, and affordable transportation services, connecting various parts of Gujarat with a wide network of buses.G.S.R.T.C offers a range of services including intercity buses, luxury buses, and special buses for tourists. With a fleet of over 8,000 buses, G.S.R.T.C ensures that passengers reach their destinations comfortably and on time.
